<h1 align = "center"> Business Questions & Answers </h1>

<p>
⁉️ Which support channels correlate with poor satisfaction?
<br>✔️ The main drivers of poor satisfaction scores are coincidently the most utilized channels (Phone & Email), with the Phone channel being the only one that has a positive ratio of 0.54 as for the worst score ratio that would be the in-App channel with an average of -1.74.</p>

<p>
⁉️ Does response time actually impact CSAT—or is that assumed?
<br>✔️ The short answer is No, the data shows that response time is almost identical across both positive and negative reviews.
 </p>
 
<p>
⁉️ Are repeat customers more forgiving than first-time buyers?
<br>✔️ Yes, loyal/returning customers have an overall higher satisfaction rate and a lower un-satisfaction rate.
</p>
<p>
⁉️ Do delivery delays hurt NPS equally across product categories?
<br>✔️ Yes, we can see that the overall delays are the same across all products.
</p>
<p>
<br>⁉️ Which are high value customers and what is the risk of losing them?
<br>✔️ 60% of the highest value customers are from the United States and 30% are from Germany, the risk of losing them is high as their average score comes up to a -0.22.
</p>
